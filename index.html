Who are we?

Media Club is the official campus student media body of IIT BHU.It aims to provide information about every events and happenings inside the college unfiltered and without any biasing.It also emphasises on inculcating the skills of content writing and design among students by holding many competitions.Our club's annual fest that is FMC Weekend is held every year in the week of february.

Fresher's Guide

Congratulations freshers!!! You've cleared the famed JEE exams and you're finally here in one of the prestigious IITs. Homesick yet? Don't know how to get to class in this bigger than Jupiter campus? Wondering whom to call when you're sick? Want to know how to change your branch? Worry not! MEDIA CLUB is here to answer all of your questions with the much awaited Freshers' Survival Guide :) It's a shame to be in the holy city of Varanasi and this 100 year old college and not explore it's depths. But where should you start exploring? We've got that covered! We bring you places to visit in Varanasi and a campus map in case you get lost XD. This last but not the least part of FSG is topped up with IIT lingo and Emergency contacts as well! Do let us know about how much you enjoyed this guide.
